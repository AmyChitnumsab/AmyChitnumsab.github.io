Alien Invasions Game
December 2021
For the final project of my "Introduction to Computing with Python" course, my partner and I worked on the "Alien Invaders" game. This game is similar to the "Space Invaders" game in which the player has to shoot all of the aliens before getting killed. The video on the right is a demostration of one full game of our version.
We implemented the functions needed to make the controls and movements of the characters work. The functions were grouped based on the model-view-controller (MVC) pattern.
